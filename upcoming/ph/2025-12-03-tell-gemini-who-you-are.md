# Tell Gemini who you are

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/tell-gemini-who-you-are*  ·  *Published: 2025-12-03*

---

Unlike [ChatGPT](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/chatgpt-memory) and [Claude](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/claude-reference-past-chats), Gemini does not automatically "get to know you" over time.

So, I strongly recommend adding some "about me" information to the Gemini "[saved info](https://gemini.google.com/saved-info)" screen.

Some things I've told Gemini:

1. What I'm working on and why.
2. Response style preferences.
3. My core strengths, weaknesses and competencies (e.g. software development).
4. Abbreviations I often use (80K, T3A, IWR, etc.).
5. I have a MacBook and a Google Pixel phone.

To make this easy, [ask ChatGPT or Claude to write a "who am I?" primer](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/llm-who-am-i) for Gemini.

See [here](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/about-me-doc) for a longer list of things you might want to share.
