## Apps I like

1. **Voice input:** [Wispr Flow](https://wisprflow.ai/). I use voice input dozens of times per day. To see how, watch this [two-minute screencast](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/voice-input-with-wisprflow).
2. **Shortcuts:** I've released [BetterChat](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/betterchat-keyboard-short/kifkmdocmmaghngfoklkgfbfkdjiihik), which adds keyboard shortcuts to ChatGPT.
3. **Call transcription:** [Granola](https://www.granola.ai/) for video calls, and the Google Pixel "[Recorder](https://recorder.google.com/)" app for in-person meetings. Granola summaries are fine, but o3 does it better, so I often copy-paste.
4. **ChatGPT o3:** Currently the best model for most of my purposes. I routinely send identical prompts to o3, Claude 4 Opus, Gemini 2.5 Pro and Grok 4 Heavy. At least two-thirds of the time, o3 wins. But it's still worth sending important stuff to several models.

## Good to know

1. **Web search:** ChatGPT o3 has [much better web search](https://www.interconnects.ai/p/summertime-outlook-o3s-novelty-coming?open=false#%C2%A7o-as-a-technical-breakthrough-beyond-scaling) than Claude 4 Opus and Gemini 2.5 Pro.
2. **Google Sheets:** There's an `=AI()` function. E.g. `=AI("categorise sentiment as positive or negative", A2)`. Docs [here](https://support.google.com/docs/answer/15877199).
3. **Data analysis:** [clean CSVs first and use o3 or Gemini](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/data-analysis). Do not use Claude.

## Try this

1. **Context:** [Make an "about me" doc](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/about-me-doc), to tell the AI who you are. This enables much better chats about planning, personal challenges, life dreams, and much else. ⭐
2. **Nutrition:** [Low-effort diet tracking](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/diet-tracking): take a photo, get the numbers (and some great advice).
3. **Vibe-coding**: [Make websites better with a personal browser extension](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/ph-mods).
4. **Voice input**: Mentioned above. But seriously: if you haven't tried it recently, [do it now](https://wisprflow.ai/). ⭐
