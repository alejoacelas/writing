# It’s easy to make multi-agent workflows with OpenAI Agent Builder

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/openai-agent-builder*  ·  *Published: 2025-10-13*

---

[OpenAI Agent Builder](https://platform.openai.com/docs/guides/agent-builder) is very easy to use.

I was able to build a multi-stage workflow with GPT-5 Pro in just a couple of minutes.

It competes with [Lindy](https://lindy.ai), [Make](https://make.com/) and [Zapier](https://zapier.com/ai). It has a much smaller feature set, but it's easier to play with.
