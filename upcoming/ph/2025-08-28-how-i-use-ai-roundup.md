# Collection of "how I use AI" links

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/how-i-use-ai-roundup*  ·  *Published: 2025-08-28*

---

To use AI, you need to _ideas_ for how to use it.

So here's a roundup of people describing what they're up to. Roughly half came via [Gavin Leech](https://www.gleech.org/llms).

## Blog posts

### Top tier

- [Andy Masley](https://andymasley.substack.com/p/how-i-use-ai) (April 2025) ⭐
- [Andrej Karpathy ](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EWvNQjAaOHw) (February 2025) ⭐
- [Nicholas Carlini](https://nicholas.carlini.com/writing/2024/how-i-use-ai.html) (August 2024) ⭐
- [Olivia Moore](https://x.com/omooretweets/status/1955638127609176346?t=XZc21J6ObJWZ8qekOFsvDQ) (August 2025) ⭐
- Peter Hartree: ["How 'Team Hartree' has helped me"](https://80000hours.org/2025/04/to-understand-ai-you-should-use-it-heres-how-to-get-started/#3-how-team-hartree-has-helped-me) (April 2025), and the journal you're reading right now. ⭐
- Simon Willison: [AI-assisted search](https://simonwillison.net/2025/Apr/21/ai-assisted-search/), [Claude artefacts](https://simonwillison.net/2024/Oct/21/claude-artifacts/) and [how I use LLMs for code](https://simonwillison.net/2025/Mar/11/using-llms-for-code/) (October 2024 - March 2025) ⭐
