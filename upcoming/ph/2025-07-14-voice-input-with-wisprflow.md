# How I use voice input

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/voice-input-with-wisprflow*  ·  *Published: 2025-07-14*

---

I use voice input dozens of times per day on my laptop.

[Wispr Flow](https://wisprflow.ai/) is [the best option for most people](https://x.com/peterhartree/status/1938531204195668154).[1](#user-content-fn-1)

On mobile, I use the voice input on ChatGPT and Claude apps more often than typing. I like to walk while I talk.

For longer walking dictations (e.g. [a week review](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/week-plans-and-reviews)), I use the Google Pixel [Recorder](https://recorder.google.com/) app. The ChatGPT and Claude apps have a dictation limit of under 10 minutes, and both are somewhat buggy (sometimes loosing the entire dictation if your connection is flaky).

## Footnotes
