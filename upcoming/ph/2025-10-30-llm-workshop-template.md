# Template for running an LLM workshop

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/llm-workshop-template*  ·  *Published: 2025-10-30*

---

[Sam Haskell](https://shmvb.substack.com/) recently ran an LLM workshop at the 80,000 Hours team retreat. He's kindly shared the [template](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Rmobr6T5xzjv6SIQjGDaLIExtL4bIBbkBDAOp6ykqp0/edit?tab=t.0) he used.

One-off workshops are nice, but continuous learning is The Way. Consider hosting events like this every week, and making attendance mandatory.
