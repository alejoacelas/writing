# Power user? Give your AI a dedicated monitor.

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/llm-screen*  ·  *Published: 2025-11-06*

---

I now use LLMs enough that it's worth having them visible most of the time. So: I've ended up with a screen that's mostly just for them.

This is especially useful for work sessions that involve long-running tasks like coding, GPT-5 Pro, and now [Claude Skills](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/claude-skills). It's also just nice to see regular chat threads alongside whatever I'm working on.
