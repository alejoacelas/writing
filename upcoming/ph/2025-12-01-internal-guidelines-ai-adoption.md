# Example of internal guidelines on AI use

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/internal-guidelines-ai-adoption*  ·  *Published: 2025-12-01*

---

Coefficient Giving (formerly: Open Philanthropy) have shared their [internal staff guidelines on using AI](https://coefficientgiving.notion.site/Public-Copy-Using-AI-at-Coefficient-Giving-2b386d14554080889e41edbaa2dbc311).

The document includes:

- Advice for people who are early on their AI adoption journey.
- Clear policies on AI usage, including a whitelist of approved software tools.
- Ways to dive deeper (e.g. book a call with an AI adoption champion).

Your org should probably have a doc like this.
