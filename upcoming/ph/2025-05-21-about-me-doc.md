# Telling the AI who I am

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/about-me-doc*  ·  *Published: 2025-05-21*

---

Sometimes I want LLMs to know a lot about me.

The [spring upgrade](https://www.theverge.com/news/646968/openai-chatgpt-long-term-memory-upgrade) to ChatGPT memory helps with this, but I want to go further.

So, I've made an "About Me" Google Doc. I add this to conversation or project context whenever I think it'll help (e.g. during my [weekly review](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/week-plans-and-reviews)).
