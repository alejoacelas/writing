# Keyboard shortcut to summarise blog posts, PDFs, YouTube videos and Google Docs

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/such-summary*  ·  *Published: 2026-04-08*

---

I've just released [Such Summary](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/such-summary/bjjhochjjajendoblpiiiebpmdjkcfcd): a browser extension for summarising blog posts, PDFs, YouTube videos and Google Docs.

The summarisation prompts ask Claude to:

- Assume the reader is PhD-level, well read in AI/ML, EA, longtermism, philosophy, and adjacent domains.
- Use standard acronyms wherever possible (and do not define them).
- Elide obvious contextual filler or hand-holding.

The prompts can be customised in settings.

This was previously one of the most-used features of [my personal browser extension](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/ph-mods). Try it, and [let me know](mailto:wow@pjh.is) how you get on! [1](#user-content-fn-1) [2](#user-content-fn-2)
