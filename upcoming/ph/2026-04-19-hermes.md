# Hermes is a big upgrade on OpenClaw

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/hermes*  ·  *Published: 2026-04-19*

---

[Hermes Agent](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/) beats OpenClaw due to:

- Better learning mechanisms (especially: [auto-generated skills](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/docs/user-guide/features/skills/#agent-managed-skills-skill%5Fmanage-tool)).
- Better [permission guards](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/docs/user-guide/security/#dangerous-command-approval).
- Easier to reason about (smaller codebase and [good documentation](https://hermes-agent.nousresearch.com/docs/user-guide/features/overview)).

It works well with Slack and Discord.[1](#user-content-fn-1) You can create channels for each major project, and Hermes replies in a thread, rather than polluting the top-level stream.

The migration tool made it easy to switch.

## Footnotes

1. I chose Discord over Slack because I want to work in my agent workspace without seeing Slack notifications. [↩](#user-content-fnref-1)
