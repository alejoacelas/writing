# Let Claude access your call transcripts

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/give-claude-call-transcripts*  ·  *Published: 2025-12-03*

---

If Claude can read your call transcripts, it can help with [week reviews](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/week-plans-and-reviews), task extraction, preparing for future calls, and so on.

This is easy with some transcription services, and harder for others. Instructions by platform below.

## Granola

[Connect Granola to Notion](https://help.granola.ai/article/notion), and have it save your call summaries there. Then, [connect Notion to Claude](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/how-to-connect-notion-to-claude).

With that setup, you'll have to click the "Share to Notion" button after every call.

## Fireflies

Open your [Claude Connector settings](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors), then click "Browse connectors > Fireflies > Connect". Every transcript is automatically available to Claude. Easy!

## Google Meet

Tell Claude to search your Google Drive for call transcripts.
