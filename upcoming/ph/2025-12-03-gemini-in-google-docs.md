# Google Docs: the Gemini assistant is finally not entirely useless

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/gemini-in-google-docs*  ·  *Published: 2025-12-03*

---

In late-November, the Gemini sidebar assistant got a [major update](https://workspaceupdates.googleblog.com/2025/11/introducing-gemini-3-pro-for-gemini-app.html).

It now uses Gemini 3 Pro, and it's good!

Some early observations...

First: document summaries are now solid.

Second: it's great to have top-tier Gemini intelligence right next to my document. For example, here it gave me several usable examples, written in almost exactly the style I wanted:

Third: the inline editor is now fast and reliable for basic text manipulation: [1](#user-content-fn-1)

There are still major limitations [2](#user-content-fn-2), UI design problems, and much else to complain about.

But: it's finally above the bar to use.

You'll need a Google Workspace plan, or an individual [Google AI Pro subscription](https://one.google.com/about/plans?g1%5Flanding%5Fpage=0).
