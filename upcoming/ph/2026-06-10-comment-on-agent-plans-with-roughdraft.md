# How to write Google Docs-style comments on agent plans

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/comment-on-agent-plans-with-roughdraft*  ·  *Published: 2026-06-10*

---

When an agent hands me a plan, I want to read it, leave comments (_à la_ Google Docs), and then have the agent read those comments and do another round of work.

[Roughdraft](https://www.roughdraft.md/) enables this.

When your agent writes a plan in Markdown, it opens the Roughdraft editor:

You leave comments (and suggested edits), then hit "Done". The agent reads them, and starts another round of work.
