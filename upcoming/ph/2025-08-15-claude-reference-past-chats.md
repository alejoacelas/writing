# Claude can now reference previous chats

*Source: https://wow.pjh.is/journal/claude-reference-past-chats*  ·  *Published: 2025-08-15*

---

**Update:** Claude now has a [memory](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/11817273-using-claude-s-chat-search-and-memory-to-build-on-previous-context#h%5Fc1c0b33879) feature, in addition to the "reference past chats" feature described below. Memory is currently available on Team and Enterprise plans only.

---

Claude can now [reference your past conversations](https://support.anthropic.com/en/articles/10185728-understanding-claude-s-personalization-features).

Think of this as "a tool you can use", not "Claude is getting to know me".

You can see when Claude is using this tool:

I succesfully requested the titles and bullet summaries of:

- All our conversations about personal finance.
- Most recent conversation about personal finance.
- My 50 most recent conversations.
- Conversations from the last 7 days.
- All conversations within a particular project.

You can also ask things like "What were we working on last week?" or "Remind me of the AI timeline cruxes between me, Jack and Jane".

Claude usually notes the conversation dates and links to the relevant thread.

**Contrast:** The ChatGPT [memory](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/chatgpt-memory) feature _should_ be thought of as "ChatGPT is getting to know me".

**Availability:** Claude Max, Team and Enterprise plans. Coming to Claude Pro soon.
