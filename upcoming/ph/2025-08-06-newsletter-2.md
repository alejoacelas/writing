## Apps I like

1. **Terminal:** [Warp](https://www.warp.dev/) is [a terminal you can talk to](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/warp-llm-terminal). Developers should try it.
2. **Narration:** [ElevenReader](https://elevenreader.com/) may be the least bad way to listen to articles and Google Docs. Here's a quick way to [make complex texts more listenable](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/raycast-format-text-for-tts).
3. **Shortcuts:** [BetterChat](https://hartreeworks.org/better-chat) now works with Claude.

## Good to know

1. **Airtable:** ["field agents"](https://support.airtable.com/v1/docs/using-airtable-ai-in-fields) can analyse documents and get information from the web. So if you have a spreadsheet of CVs or website URLs, you can use LLMs to assess them right within the sheet.
2. **Claude & Asana:** You can create Asana tasks from text or screenshots. With a bit of [config](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/create-asana-tasks-claude), this works well on desktop and mobile.
3. **Todoist:** The desktop app can [create tasks with voice](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/todoist-create-tasks-voice).
4. **Raycast:** [Raycast AI](https://www.raycast.com/core-features/ai) enables fast text manipulation, which is great for [proofreading](https://wow.pjh.is/journal/raycast-ai-proofreading).
