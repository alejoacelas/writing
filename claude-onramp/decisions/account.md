# Team or personal account? Which tier?

**Recommendation: if your organization gives you a Claude Team seat, use it —
and make sure it's a seat that includes Cowork and Claude Code. If you're on
your own, get a Max plan.**

## The short version

- **Team account** — the company pays, IT manages seats, and you get single
  sign-on and shared billing. This is the right default for most people reading
  this, because most of you are at an org that's rolling Claude out.
- **Personal Max** — you pay, and you get the highest usage ceiling of any
  individual plan. This is what independent power users pick.

Both give you the full model lineup and both work for everything in this guide.
The real differences are *who administers it* and *how much you can use before
hitting limits*.

## The one thing to check on a Team plan

Not every Team seat includes the agent tools. As of mid-2026, **Cowork and the
richest usage come with the premium Team seats, not the standard ones.** If your
Team account can't open Cowork, that's why — ask whoever manages your Claude
account to upgrade your seat, or fall back to a personal Pro/Max plan.

## The tiers, roughly

| Plan | ~Price | Gets you |
|---|---|---|
| Free | $0 | Chat only — **no Claude Code, no Cowork** |
| Pro | ~$20/mo | Unlocks Claude Code + Cowork; modest usage |
| Max 5× | ~$100/mo | Same features, ~5× the usage |
| Max 20× | ~$200/mo | ~20× the usage — heavy Claude Code users |
| Team | ~$25–125/seat/mo | Pro-level features + SSO, admin, shared billing (Cowork on premium seats) |
| Enterprise | Custom | Team + governance, audit logs, compliance |

*Prices and what's bundled change often — [check claude.com/pricing](https://claude.com/pricing)
for today's numbers.*

## If both matter

Some people at organizations keep **both**: a Team seat for the work the company
governs and bills, and a personal Max 20× for their own heavy Claude Code use,
because a single Max seat can carry more raw usage than a standard Team seat.
It's not either/or if your usage justifies it — but start with the Team seat and
add Max only if you actually hit its limits.
